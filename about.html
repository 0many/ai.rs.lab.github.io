About Us

We are ai.rs.lab

The ai.rs.lab develops machine-learning and deep-learning methods for analyzing Earth-observation and geospatial data. By applying CNNs, transformer models, and modern detection techniques to multispectral, SAR imagery, the lab builds AI systems that extract dependable environmental insights from large satellite datasets.

Research areas include rice-yield estimation, paddy-field mapping, drought and soil-moisture assessment, water-body detection, land-cover classification, and wildfire damage analysis—supporting sustainable land management and environmental monitoring.

Focusing on practical and deployable AI, the ai.rs.lab collaborates across disciplines and contributes to advancing next-generation Earth-intelligence technologies for environmental and societal benefit.
