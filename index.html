Research Areas

We are focused on developing cutting-edge AI technologies to analyze geospatial data, enhancing our understanding of the planet and enabling predictive capabilities for a sustainable future.

public

AI for Geospatial Intelligence

Developing advanced AI models to extract actionable intelligence from satellite imagery, enabling smarter decision-making in various sectors.

fullscreen

Satellite Image Super-Resolution

Enhancing the resolution and quality of satellite images using deep learning techniques, unlocking new levels of detail and analysis.

eco

Environmental Monitoring

Utilizing AI to monitor environmental changes, from deforestation and climate impact to urban sprawl and agricultural trends.
